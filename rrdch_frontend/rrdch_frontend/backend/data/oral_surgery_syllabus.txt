# RRDCH MDS Oral Surgery Syllabus & Requirements

## Introduction
The MDS in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery at Rajarajeswari Dental College & Hospital (RRDCH) is a three-year intensive program.

## Requirements for Admission
- BDS degree from a recognized university.
- Permanent registration with the Dental Council of India (DCI).
- Qualifying score in NEET MDS entrance examination.

## Course Curriculum
### Year 1: Basic Sciences and Fundamentals
- Applied Anatomy
- Physiology
- General & Oral Pathology
- Biostatistics & Research Methodology

### Year 2: Clinical Specialties
- Minor Oral Surgery
- Maxillofacial Trauma
- Orthognathic Surgery
- Implantology

### Year 3: Advanced Training & Dissertation
- Cleft Lip & Palate Surgery
- Oncology & Reconstructive Surgery
- Final Dissertation Submission

## Examination Pattern
- Theory Exams: 4 papers (100 marks each)
- Practical Exams: Clinical cases and Viva Voce
- Minimum 50% required in both theory and practicals to qualify.
